Our classic Appalachian salt n' vinegar style hot sauce. It's got that "vinegar forward" flavor, made with a 12 grain organic vinegar with the perfect balance of fresh garlic and sea salt from the coast of Maine.

Absolutely sensational on eggs, tacos, chicken, steaks, pizza, and almost anything you throw at it. A true all-purpose table sauce that earns its spot next to the salt and pepper.

Made with organically sourced chiles from Vermont farms. Zero fillers, zero xanthan gum, zero preservatives. Just real vegetables and real flavor.

Ingredients:

  • Organic cayenne peppers (Vermont-grown)
  • Organic 12-grain vinegar
  • Sea salt (Maine coast)
  • Fresh garlic
  • Onion

No xanthan gum, guar gum, starches, pepper mash, HFC corn syrup, artificial flavors, or preservatives.
