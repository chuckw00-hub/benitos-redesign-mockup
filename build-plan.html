Benito's Hot Sauce · Morrisville, VT

Website Rebuild Plan

A brand-new look for benitoshotsauce.com — built on the store you already have. Same products, same checkout, same orders. Just a site that finally looks as good as the sauce.

1. The short version

Your store already runs on Shopify and it works — all 53 products, checkout, payments, shipping. None of that gets touched. We build the new design as a new theme on your existing store: you preview it privately, request changes, and when you're happy, going live is one click. The old look stays saved, so it's reversible at any time.

2. What gets built

HomepageFrom the concept: big Vermont hero, best sellers, your story, new arrivals, "How Hot Do You Like It?" heat browser, recipes, farm partners, email signup.
Product pagesOne great template for all 53 products: large photos, heat scale, flavor tags, add-to-cart, related products. Works for sauces, syrups, and packs.
CollectionsHot Sauces · Maple Syrups · Bundles & Gifts — browsable and filterable (by heat, by type).
Cart & checkoutCart styled to match; checkout stays Shopify's secure checkout your customers already use.
The restAbout, Contact, Shipping & Returns, and a Recipes hub — restyled to match the new brand look.
Phone-firstEvery page is checked on phone and desktop before you ever see it.

3. What does NOT change

  • Your products, prices, variants, and inventory — untouched, nothing re-entered
  • Checkout, payments, taxes, and shipping — exactly as today
  • Your domain (benitoshotsauce.com), customer accounts, and discount codes
  • Existing page addresses — links and search rankings carry over
  • Orders keep flowing the whole time — there is no downtime

4. How it rolls out

  1. Access. You send a collaborator invite from your Shopify admin (Settings → Users → Collaborators). No passwords change hands, and you can revoke it anytime.
  2. Build. We convert the approved design into your store's new theme — it sits unpublished on your store while your current site keeps running.
  3. Preview. You get a private preview link of YOUR store in the new design — real products, real prices. Click around on your phone, send us everything you want changed.
  4. Revise. We turn your feedback around in short rounds until it's right.
  5. Launch. One click publishes it. The old theme stays saved as an instant fallback.

5. What we need from you

  • The collaborator invite (step 1 above)
  • About 30 minutes: which products to feature, any copy you want changed, and any photos you love or hate
  • Your honest reaction to the two concept pages — before we build, not after

6. Timeline

About 1–2 weeks from access to launch-ready, including your feedback rounds. Most of that is polish — the design direction is already done.
